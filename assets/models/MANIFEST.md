# 3D Model Manifest - Greek Odyssey Voyage Scene

All models are GLB (glTF 2.0 binary), low-poly / flat-shaded, and verified valid
(magic bytes "glTF", version 2, declared length matches file size, JSON chunk present).

Total: 12 files, 1,192,884 bytes (about 1.14 MB). Budget was 5 MB.

Art direction: stylized low-poly, warm amber accents on dark charcoal, marble-white
architecture. Every model here was visually checked against that direction before download.

## Not sourced (builder should generate procedurally or skip)

- Arch: no acceptable low-poly Greek arch found under CC0/CC-BY. Generate procedurally
  or compose from column.glb copies with a lintel beam.
- Anvil / blacksmith forge: not searched to completion; not thematically central. Skip or generate.
- Campfire / brazier: available inside a larger Poly Pizza villa scene but not as a clean
  standalone CC0/CC-BY prop. Recommend a small procedural glowing brazier for the amber accent.
- Ancient Greek trireme/galley specifically: none in acceptable quality/license. The Quaternius
  Sail Ship (ship.glb) is used as the hero instead and reads as a classical wooden sailing vessel.
